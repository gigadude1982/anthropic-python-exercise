Critical Insight

💡 Churn is driven by low engagement — not price

Users who are less engaged (watching fewer hours, shorter sessions, exploring fewer titles) are at the highest risk of churning. The data indicates that improving content discovery and personalized recommendations would have greater retention impact than pricing adjustments. Premium subscribers churn at nearly half the rate of Basic subscribers, suggesting that the value perception of higher tiers is strong once users are engaged.
